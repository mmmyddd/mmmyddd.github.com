XKeymacs: Emacs Key-Binding under Windows
#tags emacs,windows
<p>Just as Emacsplus for Eclipse, Xkeymacs is an utility for <a href="http://mmmyddd.github.io/wiki/emacs/index.html">emacs</a>-like keybindings for all
applications under windows.</p>
