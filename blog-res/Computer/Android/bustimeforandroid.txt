<p class="quoted">&quot;This is an extremely simple and easy to use application for checking bus and ferry time across entire US!&quot;</p>
</blockquote>

<ul>
<li>Get arrival times based on actual vehicle locations (powered by Next Bus)</li>
<li>Add favorite routes and stops and view their arrival times concurrently</li>
<li>Check bus stops on the map</li>
<li>See buses moving on the map in real time</li>
</ul>
